The Soul of Pizza Becod

Traditional Wood-Fire

Our hand-built brick ovens are kept at a constant 800°F. This intense heat creates the iconic "leopard-spotted" char and a crust that is airy, yet crisp.

Farm-to-Table

We don't believe in cans. Our San Marzano tomatoes are crushed daily, and our basil is picked from local greenhouses every morning at dawn.

The 48-Hour Secret

Patience is our main ingredient. Our dough undergoes a slow 48-hour cold fermentation process, resulting in a flavor profile that is deep, complex, and easy to digest.

Crafted with Love

Every pizza is hand-stretched and tossed by our master chefs. No rollers, no machines—just the human touch and a passion for authentic Italian art.
